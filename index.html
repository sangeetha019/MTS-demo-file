Course Highlights

MRCOG EDGE is an online interactive e-learning platform which would enable candidates to study as per their convenience and time schedule, and will also help candidates to pass their MRCOG exam in one go. This course is mentored by Dr. Kavitha who is the esteemed author of the bestselling books for MRCOG exams (Textbook for MRCOG-2: Basic Sciences in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, An Evidence-Based Clinical Textbook in Obstetrics and Gynaecology).
